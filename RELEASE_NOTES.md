# Traffic Light Timer v1.1.0

Traffic Light Timer grows from a simple countdown widget into a lightweight **day planner**. Plan your day in time blocks, get gentle break reminders, and keep the glanceable green → yellow → red countdown on top of everything.

## ✨ New

### 🗓️ Day Planner
- Build your day from named **time blocks**, each with a start time and duration.
- **Start any block** with one click to load its time into the timer and show it as the current task.
- **Auto-time from start** lays blocks back-to-back, automatically skipping lunch.
- Fully **adaptable mid-day** — add, rename, re-time, complete, or delete blocks at any point.
- When a block finishes you're **prompted to advance** to the next one; you stay in control and can always override.
- Today's plan is saved automatically and resets to a fresh day each morning.

### ⏰ Day Setup
- Set today's **Start, Finish, Lunch time and lunch length**.
- Save your **usual default times** and reset any day back to them with one click.

### ☕ Break Reminders
- A full-screen **break prompt** appears after every work interval (default **20 minutes**).
- Your task timer **pauses during the break** and resumes when it ends; the widget turns blue.
- Configure the **interval, break length, and message** (default break **5 minutes**), or turn breaks off.
- End early ("Back to work"), add a minute, or dismiss with **Esc**.

## 🎨 Improved
- Refreshed, **card-based control panel** with three tabs: **Timer**, **Planner**, **Settings**.
- New **current-task banner** showing what you're working on and what's next.
- Cleaner typography, spacing, buttons, and a modern colour system throughout.
- Right-click the widget to open controls; hover to see the active task.

## 🧹 Housekeeping
- Removed the unused standalone settings window (all settings now live in the control panel).
